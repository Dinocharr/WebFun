Freakazoid was an animated TV series from the 90's.
It was a story about Dexter Douglas, a nerd computer ace.
He got zapped into cyberspace, and became the insane superhero Freakazoid!
The show is a childhood favorite of mine, and I highly recommend it for fans of the Kids WB era of cartoons!
